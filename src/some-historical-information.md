title: Some Historical Information

- The concept appeared first (formally) in 1799 by Pierre-Simon Girard in France—First Patent.
- The first documented conversion of wave energy into electricity occurred in 1909 in California.
- The first Oscillating Water Column (OWC) was built around 1910 by M. Bochaux-Praceique near Bordeaux in France.
- The first successful attempt at using wave power to produce electricity took place in 1947 by Yoshio Masuda in Japan
- The idea of wave energy as a viable commercial sector gained significant momentum in the 1970s and onwards….
