title: There is an Enormous Amount of Energy in the Ocean

The estimated exploitable US wave energy is about 2,640 **Terrawatt-hours per year** (EPRI (2011), Mapping and Assessment of the United States Ocean Wave Energy Resource). This is nearly more than half the total annual electricity demand, which is about 4,000 TW/yr (EIA, ?year?).
